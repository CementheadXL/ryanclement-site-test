About Me

Bringing data, strategy, and AI to nonprofit fundraising.

Hi, I’m Ryan Clement— founder of Ryan Clement Consulting and Manager of Philanthropic Research at the Cleveland Museum of Art. With a Master’s in Business Psychology and a deep love for both data and mission-driven work, I help nonprofits tap into the power of AI and smart strategy to raise more money and work more efficiently.

I specialize in uncovering high-potential donors, building predictive models, and streamlining CRM systems, but what I really do is turn complex data into clear, actionable insight. I love partnering with teams who want to modernize their development operations and aren’t afraid to try something new.

If you're ready to grow smarter and build deeper donor relationships, I’m here to help make that happen.

Things I Can Do For You

I know from experience—especially working with small and mid-sized shops—that you may be thinking: “We need more donors,” “We have the data but aren’t sure how to use it,” or “We don’t even know what to track.” That’s completely normal. If you’d like to talk through where to start or how to move forward, just scroll down and send me a message — I’m always happy to have an initial conversation, no charge.

  • Prospect research and donor analysis to strengthen your pipeline
  • Prospect management system design and implementation that fits your team
  • Campaign modeling and predictive analytics to inform your fundraising strategy
  • Fundraising strategy and reporting that drive actionable insights
  • Training and workshops on AI tools for fundraising and prospect development
  • CRM optimization and data strategy (Tessitura, Blackbaud, iWave, RelSci, and others)

Unlocking the Power of Data and Human Connection

With a background in both front-line nonprofit work and behind-the-scenes data analysis, I understand the unique pressures small and mid-sized organizations face—especially when budgets are tight. I bring a hybrid skill set to help you do more with less: uncovering insights in your data while also strengthening how your team engages donors, constituents, and partners.
